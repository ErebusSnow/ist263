Those Who Keep Tradition Alive

Classical music is not just a part of human history; it is a living art that is passed through generations. Many contemporary pianists and composers keep these traditions alive and inspire the next generation worldwide to keep going.

Lang Lang

Lang Lang is a Chinese pianist who brings wonderful energy and showmanship to the piano. You can find videos of him throughout YouTube and Red Note, and almost every time you see the public happy to listen to his wonderful music.

Yuja Wang

Yuja Wang is another Chinese pianist celebrated for her amazing technique and bold expression. She has done multiple interviews where she states why she plays Rachmaninoff and modern repertoire her own way.

Víkingur Ólafsson

Víkingur Ólafsson is an Icelandic pianist who brings brand new interpretations of Bach and other contemporary works. His interpretations bring clarity and emotional depth, like he is not the one playing the music, but the music itself feels alive.

Daniil Trifonov

Daniil Trifonov is a Russian pianist that anyone would strive to be. He is most known for his romantic and modern repertoire interpretation, where it feels like he is speaking through these pieces.
